What is INK TALKS :

"Ink Talks" is an event dedicated to celebrating and promoting emerging writers and their works. It typically involves presentations, discussions, and competitions centered around writing, literature, and storytelling. These events aim to inspire creativity, foster community among writers, and provide opportunities for aspiring authors to showcase their talent and connect with audiences.

A Writer's Perspective

Why participate in INK TALKS ?


  1. Visibility and Exposure:

    For writers, it provides a platform to showcase their work to a wider audience, gaining exposure and potential recognition for their talent.

  2. Networking Opportunities:

    "Ink Talks" events often bring together writers, publishers, literary agents, and enthusiasts, offering valuable networking opportunities to connect with industry professionals and fellow writers.

  3. Feedback and Learning:

    writers can receive feedback on their work through competitions, discussions, and interactions with other participants, helping them improve their writing skills and gain insights into their strengths and areas for growth.

  4. Inspiration and Motivation:

    Attending "Ink Talks" can be highly inspiring, with talks, presentations, and discussions serving as sources of motivation and encouragement for writers to continue pursuing their passion for writing.

  5. Community Building:

    It fosters a sense of community among writers, providing a supportive environment where they can share experiences, exchange ideas, and form connections with like-minded individuals.

Overall, participating in "Ink Talks" can be a rewarding experience for writers seeking to grow their craft, connect with others in the literary world, and gain recognition for their work.
